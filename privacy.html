Overview

Bourbon Pourcast ("we", "us") provides bourbon drop information and alerts. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information.

Information we collect

  • Mobile number (for SMS alerts, only if you opt in)
  • Email address (if you opt in to email updates)
  • Basic technical data (e.g., browser logs) for site operation and security

How we use information

  • To send requested SMS alerts and/or email updates
  • To operate and improve the Bourbon Pourcast website and services
  • To comply with legal and carrier requirements

SMS specific

  • Message frequency varies (for drop alerts).
  • Message and data rates may apply.
  • You can opt out at any time by replying STOP.
  • You can request help by replying HELP.

Sharing

We do not sell your phone number. We may share data with service providers who help deliver messages (e.g., messaging platforms) solely to provide the service.

Data retention

We retain opt-in records and messaging logs as needed for compliance and service operation, and delete data when it is no longer needed.
