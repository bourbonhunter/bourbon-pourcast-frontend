How to interpret confidence (high-level)

Low

Possible, but not yet consistent

The store’s drop pattern is still forming or has been irregular recently. Treat as “keep an eye on it,” not a lock.

Medium

Good signal from recent history

Recent drops suggest today is a reasonable candidate. Expect some misses—this tier is where surprises still happen.

High

Strong pattern alignment

The store has a reliable cadence and a strong day-of-week signal. Not guaranteed—just the best probability bucket.
