Why this solves a real Board problem

Many boards want to create positive excitement around allocated products, but exact release announcements can create operational burdens: lines, drawings, staffing demands, arguments, and disappointed patrons. NC Allocation Signal creates a middle ground.

No PromotionQuiet shelf placement. Low burden, but low public visibility.
→
General County SignalExcitement without exact release details.
→
Exact Details Stay LocalBoards retain control over timing, stores, and operations.
Participating County ABC Boards determine their own signal and may update it whenever they choose.

Benefits to participating boards

This concept is designed around issues county boards have already raised: fairness, reliability, cost, crowd management, and operational flexibility.

Promote fairlyEveryone sees the same general signal at the same time.
Avoid drawingsNo need to run complex event-style allocation processes.
Reduce crowdsNo exact bottle/store/time information is required.
Keep controlBoards choose the status and can change it at any time.
Create goodwillGive bourbon customers a transparent, positive signal.
