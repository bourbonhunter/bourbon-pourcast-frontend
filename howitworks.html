How It Works

Each morning, we track newly allocated bourbon and determine which Wake County stores are most likely to receive a drop today—our “Pourcast.” We also include a snapshot of everything that dropped yesterday.

What you'll get for Free!
  • Yesterday’s Allocations: Daily updates from Wake County for allocations that were delivered the previous day.
  • Daily Bourbon Allocations Report: A daily inventory snapshot for your records.
What you'll get in the Premium Version!
  • Today's Pourcast: Stores that typically receive allocation drops on this weekday.
  • Daily Dashboard: A customizable dashboard of all history that lets you filter your search by store or brand.
  • Daily Bourbon Allocations Report: A daily inventory snapshot for your records.
  • Daily Drop Tracker: A daily report of allocations dropped for each Wake County store.
  • Daily NC Shipments Report: A daily report of what NC shipped to each County.
