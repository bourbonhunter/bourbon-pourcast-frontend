How It Works

Bourbon Pourcast tracks inventory changes across NC ABC stores and highlights new allocations—the moments a sought-after bottle appears in stock. Each morning, we compile a snapshot of what dropped and where, plus a pourcast of the most likely drop days ahead (based on each store’s typical pattern).

Daily Flow
  1. We scan inventories for your target brands and formats.
  2. We detect new allocations and log them to our history.
  3. We generate a daily report and a pourcast (next likely drop days).
  4. We email subscribers a clean summary with a downloadable report.
What You’ll See
  • Today’s Allocations: all stores that received new stock today.
  • Pourcast: stores grouped by their typical drop day (Mon–Fri), starting with the next business day.
  • Daily PDF: a full inventory snapshot for your records.
Get Access

Want the daily email and PDF in your inbox? Email support@bourbonpourcast.com with your name and the counties/brands you care about. We’ll get you set up.

Notes & Opt-Out
