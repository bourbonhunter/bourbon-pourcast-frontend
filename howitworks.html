How It Works

We track allocated and hard-to-find bourbon drops daily and predict which Wake County stores are most likely to receive a drop today...our “Pourcast”.

What you'll get for Free!
  • Yesterday’s Allocations: A daily email report of Wake County allocations delivered the previous day, along with an accuracy score based on what we predicted in our daily Pourcast!
  • Daily Bourbon Allocations Report: A Daily Allocations Inventory Report for your records.
What you'll get in the Premium Version!
  • Today's Pourcast: Our AI Generated Daily Pourcast for all Wake County Stores.
  • Daily Dashboard: A customizable dashboard of recent drop history that lets you filter your search by store or brand.
  • Daily NC Shipments Report: A daily report of what NC shipped to each County.
  • Daily Drop Day Tracker: A daily report of allocations dropped for each Wake County Store.
